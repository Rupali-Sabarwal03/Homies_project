Control everything in the house with your voice

A smart home with both Google Assistant and Alexa compatibility offers users the flexibility to control various smart devices using either Google Assistant or Alexa voice commands.

Key Features:

Voice-Activated Control: Utilize natural language commands to control lights, thermostat, security cameras, and more. Cross-Platform Compatibility: Whether you prefer Google Assistant or Alexa, our system works seamlessly with both platforms. Customizable Automation: Set up routines and schedules to automate tasks based on your preferences and lifestyle. Remote Access: Monitor and control your home from anywhere using your smartphone or tablet. Expandable Ecosystem: Easily integrate new smart devices into your setup as your needs evolve.

Example Commands:

"Hey Google, dim the lights in the living room." "Alexa, set the thermostat to 72 degrees." "Google, lock the front door." "Alexa, play my morning playlist."
